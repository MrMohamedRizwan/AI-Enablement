Expense Reimbursement Process - Employee Guide

OVERVIEW
========
This guide explains how to submit and track expense reimbursements for business-related expenses.

ELIGIBLE EXPENSES
=================

Travel Expenses:
- Airfare, train, bus tickets
- Hotel accommodations
- Rental cars and gas
- Taxi, rideshare, public transportation
- Parking fees and tolls

Meals and Entertainment:
- Business meals with clients/colleagues
- Conference and training meals
- Team building events
- Client entertainment (with approval)

Office and Supplies:
- Office supplies and equipment
- Software licenses and subscriptions
- Books and training materials
- Conference and seminar fees

Other Business Expenses:
- Phone and internet (if required for work)
- Professional memberships
- Certification and licensing fees
- Business insurance

EXPENSE LIMITS
==============
- Meals: $75 per day domestic, $100 per day international
- Hotel: Reasonable business rate (mid-tier hotels)
- Airfare: Economy class (business class requires pre-approval)
- Ground transportation: Most economical option
- Entertainment: $200 per event (requires manager approval)

SUBMISSION PROCESS
==================

Step 1: Gather Documentation
- Original receipts (digital photos acceptable)
- Credit card statements
- Invoices and proof of payment
- Business purpose explanation
- Attendee information (for meals/entertainment)

Step 2: Submit Through Expense System
- Log into expense portal: https://expenses.company.com
- Create new expense report
- Upload receipt images
- Enter expense details and business purpose
- Categorize expenses correctly
- Submit for approval

Step 3: Manager Approval
- Direct manager reviews and approves
- Finance reviews for policy compliance
- Additional documentation may be requested
- Approval notifications sent via email

Step 4: Reimbursement Processing
- Approved expenses processed bi-weekly
- Direct deposit to employee bank account
- Reimbursement typically within 5-7 business days
- Email confirmation when payment is issued

REQUIRED DOCUMENTATION
======================

For All Expenses:
- Original receipt or invoice
- Date and amount of expense
- Business purpose description
- Vendor/merchant information

For Meals:
- Itemized receipt (not just credit card slip)
- Names of attendees
- Business relationship of attendees
- Business purpose of meal

For Travel:
- Itinerary or booking confirmation
- Boarding passes (for flights)
- Hotel folio showing zero balance
- Mileage log (for personal vehicle use)

SUBMISSION DEADLINES
====================
- Submit expenses within 60 days of incurrence
- Monthly expense reports due by 5th of following month
- Year-end expenses must be submitted by January 15th
- Late submissions require manager approval

MILEAGE REIMBURSEMENT
=====================
- Current rate: $0.65 per mile (updated annually)
- Use company mileage tracker app
- Record start/end locations and odometer readings
- Business purpose required for each trip
- Personal commuting miles not reimbursable

CORPORATE CREDIT CARD
=====================
If you have a company credit card:
- Use for all business expenses when possible
- Submit expense reports even for corporate card charges
- Reconcile monthly statements
- Report lost/stolen cards immediately
- Personal use strictly prohibited

NON-REIMBURSABLE EXPENSES
=========================
- Personal meals and entertainment
- Alcoholic beverages (unless client entertainment)
- Traffic violations and parking tickets
- Personal travel insurance
- Spouse/family travel expenses
- First-class or business-class upgrades (without approval)
- Excessive or luxury accommodations

EXPENSE REPORT STATUS
=====================
Check status at: https://expenses.company.com
- Submitted: Awaiting manager approval
- Approved: Awaiting finance processing
- Processing: Payment being prepared
- Paid: Reimbursement completed
- Rejected: Requires correction and resubmission
